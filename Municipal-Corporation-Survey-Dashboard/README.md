# Case Study: Municipal Corporation Survey Analysis Dashboard

An interactive, single-page Power BI dashboard built to analyze voter demographics, leadership approval levels, and regional political trends. This case study maps complex public sentiment metrics across local assemblies, helping researchers, analysts, and campaign managers evaluate data points within a unified, high-density reporting view.

## 🚀 Key Features & Detailed Visual Insights

This single-page case study processes an active survey database of **13.44K unique responses**, segmented across the following analytical zones:

### 🗳️ Regional Voting Intentions
*   **Next MNC Government:** Tracks party support structures where regional parties like **Shivsena (शिवसेना)** and **BJP (भाजपा)** hold the clear majority, followed by **Congress (काँग्रेस)**, **NCP (राष्ट्रवादी)**, **MNS (मनसे)**, and **Vanchit Bahujan Aghadi (वंचित बहुजन आघाडी)**.
*   **Next Maharashtra Government & Legislative Metrics:** Maps voting shares across parliamentary groups (`Next MP`), legislative assemblies (`Next MLA`), and coalition preferences like **Mahavikas Aghadi (महाविकास...)**.

### 👤 Leadership Satisfaction Metrics
A specialized evaluation module tracks public sentiment comparing administrative leadership:
*   **समाधानी आहे (Satisfied):** Represents the dominant share of public feedback, indicating high performance sentiment.
*   **समाधानी नाही (Dissatisfied):** Identifies specific voter blocks indicating a need for policy adjustments.
*   **सांगता येत नाही (Undecided):** Isolates neutral or swing voters critical for targeted strategic outreach.

### 👥 Demographic & Community Breakdown
The data is broken down via a granular bar chart grid detailing sample sizes by social group distribution:
*   **Primary Groups:** Maratha, Other Maharashtrian, and North Indian form the largest blocks of respondents.
*   **Broad Demographic Representation:** Integrates key survey groups including Agri / Koli, Buddhist, Muslim, South Indian, and Gujrati / Jain / Rajasthani.

### 📈 Field Operations Tracking
*   An integrated agent donut chart monitors the geographical and field distribution performance handled by data collection surveyors (**Nilesh**, **Hitesh**, **Swapnil**, **Kedar**, **Paresh**, and **Saurabh**).
